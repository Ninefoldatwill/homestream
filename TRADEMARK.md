# HomeStream Trademark Usage Policy

## Our Trademarks

"HomeStream" and the HomeStream logo are trademarks of 九重工作室 (JiuChong Studio).

We are in the process of registering these trademarks under classes 9, 38, and 42.

## When You Can Use the Trademark

You may use the "HomeStream" name without written permission in these cases:

1. **Attribution** — Stating that your project "works with HomeStream" or "built on HomeStream"
2. **Education** — Tutorials, blog posts, conference talks that don't imply endorsement
3. **Open source forks** — As long as it's clear the fork is not the official distribution
4. **Community plugins/extensions** — Descriptive use like "HomeStream Discord Plugin"

## When Permission is Required

You **must** obtain written permission before:

1. Using "HomeStream" in your product, service, or company name
2. Using the HomeStream logo as your own project's logo or favicon
3. Registering domain names containing "HomeStream"
4. Creating merchandise with the HomeStream brand
5. Using the trademark in a way that implies official endorsement

## Guidelines

- **Always attribute**: "HomeStream is a trademark of 九重工作室 (JiuChong Studio)"
- **Don't modify**: Do not alter the logo, change colors, or add effects
- **Be clear**: If you're not the official project, say so explicitly
- **No confusion**: Your use should not confuse users about the origin of the software

## How to Request Permission

Email: trademark@jiuchong.studio

Include:
- Your name and organization
- How you want to use the trademark
- Where it will appear (website, app, docs, etc.)
- Duration of use

## Enforcement

We reserve the right to revoke permission if usage harms the HomeStream brand or
creates consumer confusion. This policy may be updated at any time.
